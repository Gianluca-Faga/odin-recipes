Nutella sandwich



Classic Italian nutella sandwich

Nutella sandwich is a classic afternoon snack that every kid eats during school days and summer breaks. It requires a lot of effort, so please pay extreme attention while following the recipee, otherwise you may miss some steps

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Nutella

Steps

  1. Take a butter knife, and dip it in the nutella jar
  2. Take the knife out of the jar
  3. Spread the nutella over the bread very gently, then lick whatever is left
  4. Join the slices of bread together
  5. EAT
  6. (if still hungry, repeat from point 1)
