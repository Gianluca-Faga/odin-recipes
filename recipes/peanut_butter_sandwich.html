Peanut butter sandwich



Classic American peanut butter sandwich

Peanut butter sandwich is a classic afternoon snack that every kid eats during school days and summer breaks. It requires a lot of effort, so please pay extreme attention while following the recipee, otherwise you may miss some steps

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut butter

Steps

  1. Take a butter knife, and dip it in the peanut butter jar
  2. Take the knife out of the jar
  3. Spread the peanut butter over the bread very gently, then lick whatever is left
  4. Join the slices of bread together
  5. EAT
  6. (if still hungry, repeat from point 1)
