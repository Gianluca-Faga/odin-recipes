One piece's curry rice

Ingredients:

  • 500g of beef, cut into cubes
  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and cut into cubes
  • 2 carrots, peeled and sliced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 3 tablespoons of curry powder
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 800ml of beef or chicken broth
  • Salt, to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot.
  2. Add the onion and sautÃ© until it becomes translucent.
  3. Add the beef and cook until evenly browned.
  4. Add the potatoes and carrots and mix well.
  5. Pour the beef or chicken broth into the pot and bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce the heat, cover, and simmer for about 30-40 minutes or until the meat and vegetables are tender.
  7. Add the curry powder and mix well until the curry is completely dissolved.
  8. Continue to cook for another 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  9. Adjust the salt according to your taste.
