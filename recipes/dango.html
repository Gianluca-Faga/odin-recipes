Hanami Dango

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of glutinous rice flour
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1/4 cup of hot water
  • Food coloring (pink, green, white)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the glutinous rice flour and sugar.
  2. Add hot water gradually and stir until a smooth dough forms.
  3. Divide the dough into three equal parts.
  4. Add pink food coloring to one part, green to another part, and leave the third part white.
  5. Take a small amount of dough from each color and roll them into balls.
  6. Cook the balls in boiling water until they float to the surface.
  7. Drain the balls and thread them onto skewers, alternating the colors.
