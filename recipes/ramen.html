Naruto's Ramen

Ingredients:

  • 2 packs of ramen noodles
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 200g of thinly sliced pork
  • 2 hard-boiled eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped green onions
  • 2 sheets of nori (seaweed)
  • 1 tablespoon of sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the chicken broth and set it aside.
  2. Cook the pork slices in a pan with sesame oil until well done.
  3. Cook the ramen noodles according to the package instructions.
  4. Drain the noodles and place them in bowls.
  5. Pour the hot chicken broth over the bowls with noodles.
  6. Add the cooked pork, halved hard-boiled eggs, chopped green onions, and nori.
  7. Season with soy sauce to taste.
