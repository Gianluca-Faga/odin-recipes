Rice onigiri

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of Japanese rice (sushi rice)
  • 1 sheet of nori (seaweed)
  • Choice of filling: smoked salmon, tuna, umeboshi (pickled plum)

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Japanese rice according to the package instructions.
  2. Prepare a bowl of water to moisten your hands.
  3. Take a small amount of rice in your wet hand and form a small ball.
  4. Make a small indentation in the ball and insert the chosen filling.
  5. Cover the filling with a little more rice and shape the onigiri into a triangle or round shape.
  6. Cut the nori sheet into thin strips and wrap them around the onigiri.
  7. Repeat the process for the remaining onigiri.
